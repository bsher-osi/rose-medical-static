Revolutionary Pediatric Neurology Care
in Phoenix

Rose Medical Pavilion provides expert pediatric neurology care in Phoenix at its state-of-the-art office, led by the esteemed Dr. Tamara Zach, MD, FAAN.

Dr. Tamara Zach, MD, a highly knowledgeable and caring physician, spearheads this endeavor. Her dedication to the well-being of children is evident in every aspect of the practice, from the warm and welcoming environment to the cutting-edge medical care provided. One of the key features that sets Rose Medical Pavilion apart is its emphasis on empowerment. Recognizing the challenges that parents face when dealing with pediatric neurological issues, the practice aims to equip them with the knowledge and resources needed to actively participate in their child's care. Dr. Zach and her team understand that a collaborative approach, where parents are informed and involved, leads to better outcomes for young patients.
