Conditions We Treat

Dr. Tamara Zach MD provides expert pediatric neurology care for children and adolescents throughout Phoenix and Arizona.

⚡

Seizures

Evaluation and management of first seizures, febrile seizures, and recurrent seizure disorders in children.
🧠

Epilepsy

Comprehensive epilepsy care including diagnosis, medication management, and coordination with epilepsy centers.
💊

Headaches & Migraines

Diagnosis and treatment of pediatric migraines, tension headaches, and chronic daily headache syndromes.
📊

EEG & Brain Monitoring

In-office EEG testing to evaluate brain electrical activity for seizure disorders and other neurological conditions.
🏃

Concussion & TBI

Post-concussion evaluation and return-to-play/school guidance for children and adolescents.
🔄

Tics & Tourette Syndrome

Evaluation and management of motor and vocal tic disorders, including Tourette syndrome.
👶

Developmental Delays

Neurological evaluation for children not meeting developmental milestones across speech, motor, and cognitive domains.
💪

Cerebral Palsy & Muscle Disease

Specialist care for children with cerebral palsy, neuromuscular disorders, and muscle diseases.
🔬

Brain MRI Interpretation

Expert review and interpretation of pediatric brain MRI findings in the context of each child's clinical presentation.
📅

Not Sure Where to Start?

Schedule a consultation and Dr. Zach will determine the best evaluation path for your child.
